We believe that the big traditional social media platforms have become to big for their own good. Too much power concentrated in the hands of a few oligarchs in Silicon Valley is leading to an internet that is closed and not with the best interests of the people in mind.

We also understand that network effects make it difficult for people to leave those platforms. For better or worse, these platforms still have an incredible amount of amazing content and it is hard to let go of the connections that hundreds of millions of people have made it through these platforms throughout the years.

Fediverser wants to solve these problems with a two-pronged approach. First, we want to make the content from the current platforms available in the equivalent fediverse platform. After the content is available, more people will be able to make the switch.
