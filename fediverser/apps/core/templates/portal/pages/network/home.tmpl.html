Get started in the Fediverse

The Fediverse works differently than traditional social networks, and this makes things a bit tricky. One of the biggest challenges is that the Fediverse is not one *single* site or app, but a large number of different servers. While we do believe that choice and diversity is one of the things that make the Fediverse great, it also makes you spend some time learning about the different options.

Build the most comprehensive map of communities in the Fediverse

We already recommend alternatives for thousands of the most popular subreddits. Our goal is to expand this database so that we can help everyone find other people that share the same interests as yours.
